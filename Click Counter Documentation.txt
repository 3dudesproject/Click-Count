DESCRIPTION:
This is a simple Click Counter that allows you  to increase or decrease a value to keep count.
You can also hold enter while clicking one the "+" or "-" buttons to increase or decrease the value rapidly.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EXTRA FEATURES:
The Click counter also has a dark theme (black and gold) along with the default (blue and white) light theme.
Simply click on the respective buttons to switch between themes.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Thats it!
